ð Previous research projects

I have contributed to the Institute of Data Science (IDS's) of the Maastricht University participation in several European-wide research projects. You can find more on my Github (@carlosug) or checkout my contributions on this PDF:

  • CDDI (Community of Data Driven Insights), implementing FAIR research data and software management strategies with digital techniques across research communities.
  • DSC, (Data Sharing Coalition, check press article), is a Dutch project from the Ministry of Economy aiming at increasing awareness from industry about data standards and interoperability cross-sectorial. I was particularly involved in building a knowledge base from texts about human trafficking data partnered with Sustainable Rescue NGO and Rosaka SA.
  • LD-Wizard, a framework for creating end-user focused GUIs that simplify the creation and publication of linked data relevant to digital humanities. My main contribution was to integrate common OWL Ontologies that are relevant to describe archaeological digital objects.
  • ODISSEI, testing the effects of privacy preservation on the utility of synthetic data in social-emotions usecase. I contributed to the legal WP by developing a legal framework using data science techniques that meets requirements on EU GDPR regulation and policy.
  • ESGREEN, building a KG for Open Government Data that integrates data biodiversity open data produced by governments. I helped to generate the ESGREEN KG for visualizing statistical and geospatial data using a semantically meaningful data model such Semanticscience Integrated Ontology (SIO).
As a joint researcher and data scientist at IDS , I have been always working on reproducibility to improve FAIR sharing of open research data within the European Open Science Cloud (EOSC), and EU COST Action CA19134 - Distributed Knowledge Graphs (DKG) projects as well as building communities and standards for research data and software best practices.

I had also participated in industry as data scientist in the IT department with a focus on data analysis and fusion across space sector such as European Space Agency (ESA) or Investment Sector European Investment Bank (EIB).
