## Interests

I always wanted to be a researcher, since I was a kid. For me, there is really no other path I ever seriously have contemplated (though my plan B is to become a data scientist). I truly love the sciences, especially digital history and statistics. Althought I found the quantitative part rather challenging, until later on (in university), when I decided to tackle it more seriously. It turned out to be far easier than I had expected. I also love reading human history (genome-wide ancient DNA reseach to human population gentics) and philosophy, and programming too!. I find both challenging and rewarding.

When not dealing with numbers, coding or reading, I'm a happy uncle, a swimmer and a tennis player.
